# IMAV 2025 — Indoor Search & Rescue

**3rd place, indoor competition.** Black Bee Drones' entry for the 16th International Micro Air
Vehicle Conference and Competition (IMAV 2025), San Andrés Cholula, Puebla, Mexico. The indoor
event was a Search-and-Rescue scenario: navigate a disaster zone, locate victims, deliver aid.

## What it did

- **Mission 1 — Enter the room:** find and fly through a gate (a five-gate tunnel), searching
  with a sweep pattern, aligning with a proportional visual servo, then passing through.
- **Mission 4 — Land on a moving platform with smoke:** search with the down-facing camera,
  pre-center, then descend while centering onto a 1 m platform moving at 0.1 m/s under reduced
  visibility.

Missions are sequenced by a hierarchical [Yasmin](https://github.com/uleroboticsgroup/yasmin)
state machine (initialize → takeoff → gate → navigate/avoid → platform → land).

## Stack

- **Platform:** Pixhawk 2.4.8 (ArduPilot) + Jetson Orin Nano Super.
- **Cameras:** Intel RealSense D435i (front) + IMX219 (down).
- **Navigation:** NVIDIA Isaac ROS Visual SLAM for GPS-denied pose.
- **SDK:** Nectar v0.1.0 (then named `mirela_sdk`) — drone control over MAVROS, the camera
  layer, and the obstacle-detection system used during forward navigation.
- **Detection:** Ultralytics YOLOv11n — [gate](https://huggingface.co/blackbeedrones/imav-2025-gate)
  and [platform](https://huggingface.co/blackbeedrones/imav-2025-platform) models.
