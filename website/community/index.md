# Community

Nectar SDK is built and used by [Black Bee Drones](https://github.com/Black-Bee-Drones), an
autonomous-drone competition team. Contributions, questions, and bug reports are welcome.

## Get involved

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   **Ask or discuss**

    General questions, ideas, and design discussion.

    [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/Black-Bee-Drones/nectar-sdk/discussions)

-   **Report a bug or request a feature**

    One issue per report, with steps to reproduce and your version/OS/hardware.

    [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Black-Bee-Drones/nectar-sdk/issues)

-   **Contribute code or docs**

    Setup, style conventions, tests, and the pull-request process.

    [Contributing](../project/contributing.md)

-   **See it in action**

    The competition missions built on the SDK.

    [Projects](../projects/index.md)

</div>
